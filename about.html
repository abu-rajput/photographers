Meet Anmol

My Story

Wedding Photographer & Filmmaker

For over a decade, I've had the incredible privilege of documenting love stories across India and around the world. What started as a passion for capturing candid moments has evolved into a lifelong mission to preserve the most precious memories of the most important day in people's lives.

Every wedding tells a unique story, and my approach is to blend seamlessly into your celebration, capturing the genuine emotions, spontaneous laughter, and intimate moments that make your day truly yours. I believe in authentic storytelling over posed perfection.

When I'm not behind the camera, you'll find me exploring new destinations, seeking inspiration in art and cinema, or planning the next adventure with my camera in hand.
